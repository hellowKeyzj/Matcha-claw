WeChat Operation Guide

中文教程

  1. 点击连接微信频道生成一个二维码。
  2. 用微信扫码，确认连接。扫完之后，微信里就会多出一个「微信 ClawBot」的对话，直接发消息就能和你的龙虾聊天了。

__注意：__当前为beta版本，ios系统需要更新重启，功能可以使用。仅支持单一Agent，暂时不支持多Agent。

English Guide

  1. Click “Connect WeChat Channel” to generate a QR code.
  2. Scan the QR code using WeChat and confirm the connection. After scanning, a new chat called “WeChat ClawBot” will appear in your WeChat. You can start chatting with your bot directly there.

Note: This is currently a beta version. On iOS devices, you may need to update and restart before it works properly. Only a single agent is supported at the moment; multi-agent support is not yet available.
