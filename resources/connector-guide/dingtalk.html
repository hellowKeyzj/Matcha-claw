DingTalk Operation Guide

中文教程

第一步： 点击 “创建或加入企业”

  • 点击 “企业” 后，选择 “创建企业/团体”

  • 填写信息并创建（填写 “企业名称” 后会出现如下内容需要填写，企业名称需要超过三个字，内容可随便填写，无特殊要求）

第二步：进入钉钉开发者平台创建机器人

钉钉开发者平台网址：https://open-dev.dingtalk.com

  • 选择刚刚创建的组织

  • 选择 “创建”

  • 左侧栏目选择 “机器人” 后，点击 “创建应用”

  • 填写 “应用名称” 和 “应用描述” 后点击 “保存”

  • 在左侧栏目选择 “添加应用能力” 并选择添加“机器人”

  • 开启机器人配置并填写内容

第三步：调整权限后发布即可绑定

  • 在左侧栏目选择 “权限管理”，搜索 “消息”，勾选图中两项后点击 “批量申请”

  • 在左侧栏目选择 “版本管理与发布” 后，点击创建新版本，必须创建版本信息才能够发布

  • 输入 “版本描述” 后 “保存”，即发布完成

  • 选择左侧栏目的 “凭证与基础信息”，复制 APP ID 和 Client Secret 到MatchaClaw客户端中保存并连接即可完成互联

第四步：如何找到创建的机器人

  • 在钉钉【消息】栏的“搜索框”，搜索刚刚创建的 “机器人名称”，会展示在功能栏下

English Guide

Step 1: Click “Create or Join Enterprise.”

  • Select “Create Organization/Group.”

  • Fill in the required information and create.
  • Note: After entering the “Organization Name” (must be more than three characters), additional fields will appear. You may fill these in as you wish; there are no specific requirements.

Step 2: Create a Bot in the DingTalk Open Platfor

  • Access the DingTalk Open Platform: https://open-dev.dingtalk.com.
  • Select the organization you just created.

  • Click “application development” and on the left sidebar, select “Robots” and click “Create Application.”

  • Enter the “App Name” and “App Description,” then click “Save.”

  • In the left sidebar, select “Add App Capabilities” and choose “Robot.”

  • Enable the robot configuration and fill in the required details.

Step 3: Adjust Permissions and Publish to Bind

  • Select “Permission Management” in the left sidebar. Search for “Message,” check the two items shown in the diagram, and click "Batch Request.

  • Select “Version Management & Release” in the left sidebar and click the Edit button.

  • Note: You must edit the version information before you can publish.
  • Enter a “Version Description,” click “Save,” and the release is complete.

  • Select “Credentials & Basic Info” in the left sidebar.

  • Copy the App ID and Client Secret, paste them into the MatchaClaw client, save, and connect to finalize the integration.

Step 4: How to Locate Your Robot

  • In the DingTalk “Messages” tab, go to the Search Bar. Search for the “Robot Name” you created. The robot will appear under the “Functions” section.
