# DingTalk Operation Guide

## 中文教程

## 第一步： 点击 “创建或加入企业”

![](dingtalk-images/001.png)

- 点击 “企业” 后，选择 “创建企业/团体”

![](dingtalk-images/002.png)

![](dingtalk-images/003.png)

- 填写信息并创建（填写 “企业名称” 后会出现如下内容需要填写，企业名称需要超过三个字，内容可随便填写，无特殊要求）

![](dingtalk-images/004.png)

![](dingtalk-images/005.png)

__第二步：进入__[__钉钉开发者平台__](https://open-dev.dingtalk.com/)__创建机器人__

钉钉开发者平台网址：https://open-dev.dingtalk.com

- 选择刚刚创建的组织

![](dingtalk-images/006.png)

- 选择 “创建”

![](dingtalk-images/007.png)

- 左侧栏目选择 “机器人” 后，点击 “创建应用”

![](dingtalk-images/008.png)

- 填写 “应用名称” 和 “应用描述” 后点击 “保存”

![](dingtalk-images/009.png)

- 在左侧栏目选择 “添加应用能力” 并选择添加“机器人”

![](dingtalk-images/010.png)

- 开启机器人配置并填写内容

![](dingtalk-images/011.png)

![](dingtalk-images/012.png)

![](dingtalk-images/013.png)

## 第三步：调整权限后发布即可绑定

- 在左侧栏目选择 “权限管理”，搜索 “消息”，勾选图中两项后点击 “批量申请”

![](dingtalk-images/014.png)

- 在左侧栏目选择 “版本管理与发布” 后，点击创建新版本，必须创建版本信息才能够发布

![](dingtalk-images/015.png)

- 输入 “版本描述” 后 “保存”，即发布完成

![](dingtalk-images/016.png)

- 选择左侧栏目的 “凭证与基础信息”，复制 APP ID 和 Client Secret 到MatchaClaw客户端中保存并连接即可完成互联

![](dingtalk-images/017.png)

![](dingtalk-images/018.png)

![](dingtalk-images/019.png)

![](dingtalk-images/020.png)

## English Guide

## Step 1: Click "Create or Join Enterprise."

![](dingtalk-images/027.png)

- Select __"Create Organization/Group."__

![](dingtalk-images/028.png)

![](dingtalk-images/029.png)

- Fill in the required information and create.
- *Note: After entering the "Organization Name" \(must be more than three characters\), additional fields will appear. You may fill these in as you wish; there are no specific requirements.*

![](dingtalk-images/030.png)

![](dingtalk-images/031.png)

## Step 2: Create a Bot in the DingTalk Open Platfor

- Access the __DingTalk Open Platform__: [https://open-dev.dingtalk.com](https://open-dev.dingtalk.com).
- Select the organization you just created.

![](dingtalk-images/032.png)

- Click  __"application development"__ and on the left sidebar, select __"Robots"__ and click __"Create Application."__

![](dingtalk-images/033.png)

![](dingtalk-images/034.png)

- Enter the __"App Name"__ and __"App Description,"__ then click __"Save."__

![](dingtalk-images/035.png)

- In the left sidebar, select __"Add App Capabilities"__ and choose __"Robot."__

![](dingtalk-images/036.png)

- Enable the robot configuration and fill in the required details.

![](dingtalk-images/037.png)

![](dingtalk-images/038.png)

![](dingtalk-images/039.png)

## Step 3: Adjust Permissions and Publish to Bind

- Select __"Permission Management"__ in the left sidebar. Search for __"Message,"__ check the two items shown in the diagram, and click __"Batch Request.__

![](dingtalk-images/040.png)

- Select __"Version Management & Release"__ in the left sidebar and click the __Edit__ button.

![](dingtalk-images/041.png)

- *Note: You must edit the version information before you can publish.*
- Enter a __"Version Description,"__ click __"Save,"__ and the release is complete.

![](dingtalk-images/042.png)

- Select __"Credentials & Basic Info"__ in the left sidebar.

![](dingtalk-images/043.png)

- Copy the __App ID__ and __Client Secret__, paste them into the __MatchaClaw client__, save, and connect to finalize the integration.

![](dingtalk-images/044.png)

![](dingtalk-images/045.png)

![](dingtalk-images/046.png)

## Step 4: How to Locate Your Robot

- In the DingTalk __"Messages"__ tab, go to the __Search Bar__.  Search for the __"Robot Name"__ you created.  The robot will appear under the __"Functions"__ section.
